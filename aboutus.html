We are a committed and effective activist organisation for the welfare of whales. We have been involved in the revolutionary documentary Blackfish, and campaign actively against whales in captivity.

Hunting is one of the biggest threats to whales. We work with world renowned organisations such as WWF to protect whales from hunting. Whales have faced a history of harm from commercial whaling, and we are committed to conservation to protect whales from negative human impacts.

We believe proper education is key to protecting whales. Working in collaboration with partner organisations, we educate people on what to do if they come across a stranded whale. This is key to protecting these animals and preventing further deaths.
