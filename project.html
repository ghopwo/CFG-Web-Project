Save the Whales are a UK charity dedicated to the protection of whales. Our vision is a world where every whale is free and safe. We care deeply about whales, and we share this passion with our supporters and our trustees.

    What we do:

  • We work globally and locally: through campaigns, lobbying, conservation projects and rescue. Our work is backed by highly regarded research, science and philosophy.
  • We bring together the valuable work of NGOs, governments, scientists and businesses.
  • Together we can create a world where every whale is safe and free.
